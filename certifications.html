How to Prepare

Most people need 20-40 hours of study time. Here's a straightforward approach:

  1. Complete the Microsoft Learn path. Each cert has a free learning path with modules and hands-on labs. Don't skip the exercises.
  2. Get hands-on experience. Fabric has a 60-day free trial. Azure gives $200 credit for new accounts. Use them.
  3. Take the practice assessment. Microsoft offers free practice tests. Take one, note where you score below 80%, and review those areas.
  4. Schedule the exam. Book it 1-2 weeks out through Pearson VUE. Having a deadline helps you focus.

Tips

Book first, study second. A deadline creates commitment.

Don't just read. The exams test practical skills. Complete every lab.

During the exam: Don't spend more than 2 minutes per question. Flag hard ones and come back.

If you fail: You can retake after 24 hours. Many pass on attempt two.
